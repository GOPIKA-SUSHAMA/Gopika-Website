Projects

Appointment Booking App

This application built using Power Apps, allows patients to easily book appointments with their preferred doctors. Patients can search by department or doctor name, select a time slot, and book an appointment. A confirmation is sent to the patient’s and hospital's email. Additionally, the app features a chatbot to assist with common queries and redirect the patient when needed. A Power BI dashboard is integrated for hospital management to analyze appointment data, providing insights into the most consulted doctors and departments.
